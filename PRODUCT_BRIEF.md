# PRODUCT BRIEF — Executive Concierge SP

## Executive Summary

**Executive Concierge SP** is a premium digital concierge application for high-net-worth foreign executives visiting São Paulo — primarily North Americans, secondarily Europeans — on business or bleisure trips.

The product operates as the digital extension of a premium human concierge service. It removes friction, saves time, organizes the executive's stay, delivers curated high-quality recommendations, and provides instant support through a combination of AI and human concierge agents.

This is **not** a general tourism app, not a marketplace, not a social network. It is a quiet, reliable, intelligent assistant built for people who do not have time to waste and will not tolerate mediocre experiences.

---

## Problem Space

Foreign executives in São Paulo face consistent friction:

| Problem | Impact |
|---|---|
| No trusted local knowledge | Poor decisions, wasted time |
| Generic recommendations (TripAdvisor, Google) | Low trust, irrelevant results |
| Language barriers | Frustration, execution risk |
| No integration with their actual schedule | Time lost coordinating |
| Lack of a reliable point of contact | Anxiety during the trip |
| Fragmented experience across multiple apps | Cognitive overload |

---

## Target User

**Primary:** North American executive (C-suite, VP, senior director) traveling to São Paulo for business, likely staying 3–7 days, combining meetings with some leisure.

**Secondary:** European executive with similar profile.

**Characteristics:**
- High income, high standards
- Time-poor, efficiency-obsessed
- Values discretion and privacy
- Familiar with premium services (Amex Centurion, Four Seasons concierge, etc.)
- Will pay for reliability over saving money
- English as primary language, no Portuguese

---

## Core Value Proposition

> "Your personal São Paulo concierge — organized, intelligent, always available."

- Organizes the full stay in one place
- Provides curated, context-aware recommendations (not generic lists)
- Connects instantly to a human concierge when needed
- Works with the executive's actual calendar
- Delivers a luxury-grade digital experience

---

## Business Context

- An existing premium executive transport service already operates outside the app
- Transport is NOT the MVP core — it will be integrated later as a complementary service
- The app expands the value proposition: concierge, personalization, recommendations, support
- Revenue model: subscription or bundled with the existing service (TBD — out of MVP scope)

---

## Product Principles

1. **Less is more** — every screen must justify its existence
2. **Trust first** — the user must feel safe sharing their schedule and preferences
3. **Human in the loop** — AI accelerates, humans close the loop
4. **Discrete and elegant** — never intrusive, never noisy
5. **Reliable over clever** — a good recommendation beats a wrong AI answer

---

## Success Metrics (MVP)

| Metric | Target |
|---|---|
| Onboarding completion rate | > 80% |
| Daily active sessions per trip | ≥ 2 per day |
| Concierge request resolution time | < 2 hours |
| Recommendation engagement rate | > 40% |
| User satisfaction (NPS) | > 60 |
| Session length | 3–8 min |

---

## Out of Scope (MVP)

- Brazilian users or Portuguese language interface
- General tourism features
- Marketplace with hundreds of vendors
- Social features
- Automated execution without human oversight
- Payment processing (deferred)
- Transport booking (deferred to next phase)
